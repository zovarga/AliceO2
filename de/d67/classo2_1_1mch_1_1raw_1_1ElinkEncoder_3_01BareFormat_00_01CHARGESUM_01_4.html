Detailed Description

template<typename CHARGESUM>
class o2::mch::raw::ElinkEncoder< BareFormat, CHARGESUM >

Center piece of the MCH Bare Raw Data Format encoder logic.

Converts the data from one SampaCluster into a bit stream that mimics the way Elinks see data.
