Detailed Description

Implementation of a calibrator object that checks the computed mean and RMS of the pedestals and compares the values with user-supplied thresholds. The channels whose values exceed one of the thresholds are considered bad/noisy and they are stored into a "bad channels" list that is sent to the CDDB populator.
