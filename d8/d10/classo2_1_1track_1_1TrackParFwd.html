Track parameters.

Track parameters ordered as follow: 

X       = X coordinate   (cm)
Y       = Y coordinate   (cm)
PHI     = azimutal angle
TANL    = tangent of \lambda (dip angle)
INVQPT    = Inverse transverse momentum (GeV/c ** -1) times charge (assumed forward motion)
